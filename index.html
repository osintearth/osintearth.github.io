About us

What?

OSINT Earth is a pioneering platform that serves as your comprehensive directory of public record lookup resources from all around the world. Our mission is straightforward: to provide you with easy access to websites that enable you to search for public records.

Why?

OSINT Earth understands the value of transparency and knowledge. Our platform is made to empower journalists, legal professionals, and curious minds by offering links to public record databases. We know practicing OSINT at a global scale is difficult, we're here to help

Expansion

Record Types

During this initial phase, we'll be offering access to individual and company records. We ask for your patience as we work hard to expand our data. We have a vast array of record types already in storage, waiting to be organized and parsed for future updates.

Countries

We are committed to expanding our coverage of countries and regions. We will continuously add more countries and resources to create the most extensive directory of public record lookup options ever assembled. The world's knowledge will be at your fingertips.
